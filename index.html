01. ABOUT

About Me

Lecturer (Senior Scale), Researcher, Cyber Security Advocate

Md. Umaid Hasan is currently working as a Lecturer (Senior Scale) at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Daffodil International University. He pursued his Master's and Bachelor's degrees from Daffodil International University in 2022 and 2020 respectively. His research interest is in Machine Learning, Data Science, Deep Learning, and IoT. He is also working on developing course content for Data Mining and IoT on online platforms like International Online University (IoU). He serves as the Co-Convener of the Cyber Security Club at DIU.
